What warranty options are available for garage door openers and accessories purchased in Joliet, IL?
Most garage door openers come with a manufacturers warranty, typically covering parts and motor for 1-5 years. Some models offer lifetime motor warranties. Accessories might have separate warranties, usually lasting 1 year. Its essential to check the specific warranty details provided by the manufacturer or retailer.
Can I extend the warranty on my garage door opener and accessories after purchase?
Yes, many manufacturers and retailers offer extended warranty plans for garage door openers and accessories. These plans can provide additional coverage beyond the standard warranty period, often including parts and labor. Its recommended to inquire about these options at the time of purchase.
What should I do if my garage door opener or accessory needs repair under warranty in Homer, Illinois?
If your garage door opener or accessory requires repair under warranty, contact the retailer or manufacturer directly to initiate a claim.
