Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I perform maintenance on my garage door opener?
Its recommended to perform maintenance on your garage door opener at least once a year to ensure optimal performance and longevity.
What are the key components to inspect during a garage door opener maintenance check?
Key components include the tracks, rollers, springs, cables, and the opener mechanism itself. Check for wear, lubrication needs, and proper alignment.
How can I ensure my garage door opener’s safety features are functioning correctly?
Test the auto-reverse feature by placing a small object in the door’s path. The door should reverse immediately upon contact. Also, check the photo-eye sensors for alignment and cleanliness.
What lubrication should I use for my garage door opener and its parts?
Use a silicone-based lubricant or a garage door-specific lubricant for the opener’s moving parts, such as rollers, hinges, and tracks, to reduce friction and noise.
When should I consider replacing my garage door opener instead of repairing it?
Consider replacing your opener if it’s over 10-15 years old, frequently malfunctions, lacks modern safety features, or doesn’t operate smoothly despite regular maintenance.
